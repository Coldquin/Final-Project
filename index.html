About Us

Welcome to our family-run holiday home and hostel on the beautiful Achill Island! Managed by two brothers, we offer a warm and welcoming stay in one of Ireland's top spots for surfing and hiking. Check in with us for the Achill half marathon and The Battle of the lake. Come and experience the adventure and beauty of Achill Island with us!"

Check out Blue Flag beaches on the island

Keel

Renowned for its consistent surf conditions, Keel Beach offers a range of waves suitable for all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned pros. Even if you're not into surfing, the beach is a fantastic spot to soak up the stunning natural beauty of the island,

Doega

Dooega Beach on Achill Island is a hidden gem, boasting pristine sands and crystal-clear waters. It's a tranquil escape, surrounded by stunning landscapes and offering a peaceful day by the sea.

Keem Bay

Keem Bay, nestled on the western edge of Achill Island, is a breathtaking haven of natural beauty. This Blue Flag beach boasts pristine sands, crystal-clear waters, and dramatic cliffs that rise majestically from the shore.
